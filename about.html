About the Booster Club

Who We Are

Supporting Every Student. Every Note.

The Bradwell Band Booster Club is a parent and community volunteer organization dedicated to supporting the full Bradwell Institute Band program — including our Marching Band, Concert Band, Symphonic Band, and Jazz Ensemble. We fund equipment, travel, uniforms, competitions, and enrichment opportunities that make a great band program possible.
