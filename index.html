Who We Are

The Bradwell Band Booster Club

The Bradwell Band Booster Club is a parent and community volunteer organization dedicated to supporting the full Bradwell Institute Band program — from the marching field to the concert hall.

We fund equipment, travel, uniforms, and enrichment opportunities that make a great band program possible. Our members show up at every performance, fundraiser, and community event — because we believe music education shapes students for life, not just for the season.
